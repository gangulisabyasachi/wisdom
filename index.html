WISDOM: A Multidisciplinary Research Journal

WISDOM is a peer-reviewed, multidisciplinary journal committed to the advancement of academic knowledge and the promotion of innovative research. Covering diverse fields of study, the journal serves as a dynamic platform for scholars, researchers, and professionals to publish original articles, critical reviews, and evidence-based studies.

With a focus on academic excellence, originality, and global relevance, WISDOM aims to encourage cross-disciplinary dialogue, foster intellectual growth, and contribute meaningfully to contemporary research and practice. By providing open access to quality scholarship, the journal supports the exchange of ideas and strengthens the worldwide academic community.
