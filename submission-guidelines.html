Submission Guidelines

Comprehensive guidelines for authors submitting manuscripts

1

Manuscript Preparation

  • Manuscripts should be 3,000-8,000 words
  • Use APA 7th edition citation style
  • Submit in Microsoft Word (.docx) format
  • Include abstract (200-300 words)
  • Provide 4-6 keywords
  • Double-space throughout the manuscript
  • Use 12-point Times New Roman font
  • Number all pages consecutively
2

Review Process

  • Double-blind peer review
  • Initial review within 2-3 weeks
  • Final decision within 8-12 weeks
  • Plagiarism check mandatory
  • Ethics approval required for human subjects
  • Statistical analysis verification
  • Editorial board final approval
3

Manuscript Structure

  • Title page with author details
  • Abstract with keywords
  • Introduction with literature review
  • Methodology section
  • Results and analysis
  • Discussion and conclusions
  • References in APA format
  • Appendices (if applicable)
4

Submission Requirements

  • Cover letter with research significance
  • Author biographical statements
  • Conflict of interest declaration
  • Data availability statement
  • Copyright transfer agreement
  • Ethics committee approval (if applicable)
  • Funding acknowledgment details
5

Figures and Tables

  • High-resolution images (300 DPI minimum)
  • Submit figures as separate files
  • Tables in Word format, not images
  • Clear, descriptive captions
  • Numbered consecutively
  • Referred to in the text
  • Copyright permissions for reused content

Ready to Submit?

Submit your manuscript through our online submission system. Ensure all requirements are met before submission.

Publication Timeline

Submission to First Decision

  • Initial editorial screening: 1-2 weeks
  • Peer review process: 4-6 weeks
  • Editorial decision: 6-8 weeks

Acceptance to Publication

  • Author revisions: 2-4 weeks
  • Final review: 1-2 weeks
  • Copyediting and proofing: 2-3 weeks
  • Online publication: 1 week
